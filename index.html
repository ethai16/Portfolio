Hello! I'm Erick

I am a Full Stack Web Developer and Software Engineer from Houston, TX. Achieving a GPA of 3.76, I graduated Magna Cum Laude from the University of Houston with a Bachelors of Science in Kinesiology and Exercise Science in 2016. Following my undergraduate career, I was accepted to the Doctor of Physical Therapy program at the University of Texas Medical Branch in Galveston,TX where I met many amazing individuals. My time spent there was very unique and rewarding, however, I began to realize that the profession was not truely for me. I initially believed I wanted to pursue a career in the food industry because of my interests in the culinary arts, but in reality, I loved the art of creation.

As an avid gamer, I quickly embraced the idea of software engineering. I wanted to learn development and create something to call my own. With many ideas flowing through my mind, I knew exactly what my next step was, learn to code. With each passing day since, the gratification I achieved from the multiple projects I completed is immense. I can honestly say, I can't wait to learn MORE!
